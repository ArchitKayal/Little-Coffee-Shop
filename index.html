Coffee Trivia

When did coffee first appear in the India?

According to legend, a Sufi saint named Baba Budan smuggled seven coffee beans from Yemen while on a pilgrimage to Mecca and brought them back to India in the 17th Century. (Source: Daily Coffee News)
